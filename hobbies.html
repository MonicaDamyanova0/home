Art

I've been drawing since I was very little, since I could really remember. It's probably the hobby I do the most and the thing that I'm best at. I draw basically everyday even if it's just a few small doodles. I typically do more digital art now since it's much quicker for me and I don't have to clean up anything afterwards. Sometimes I also do traditional art, but not as frequently as I used to.

3D Modeling

Along with drawing, I've also tried some 3D modeling. I learned how to do it during the summer of 2020. I made a few different models which was a lot of fun, yet kind of challenging. There are lots of parts to 3D modeling you need to learn like the actual modeling bit, rigging, making textures, etc. I haven't model much recently since it takes quite a while to do.

Animation

I started animating some time around grade 5 when I was able to mess around with Photoshop. For a while I had wanted to learn how to animate and I had seen other people animating with Photoshop so I though I might as well try to. It was a fun experience and continued animating when I found the time to. I don't use Photoshop to animate anymore though, I use FlipaClip and Procreate instead.

Video Editing

For a long time I would solely use animation programs to make and finalize my animations which would take a while, so I thought if I were to use a video editing program, I could probably do it faster. So I downloaded DaVinci Resolve and started learning how to use it. I haven't made a lot with it yet, but I plan on doing more.
